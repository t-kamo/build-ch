# Research Summary

## Research Conclusion

The strongest repeatable video structure is:

Before -> Build Process -> Final Reveal

The most viral hooks are:

- Secret
- Hidden
- Underground
- Abandoned
- Transformation
- Impossible location

## Recommended Core Concept

Hidden Build Transformation

Create short vertical AI-generated construction videos where ordinary, empty, abandoned, or impossible locations are transformed into hidden homes, secret bases, underground bunkers, or luxury survival spaces.

## Winning Structure

1. Show an empty, abandoned, or impossible location
2. Introduce a clear hidden construction goal
3. Show fast step-by-step construction
4. Increase completion level every few seconds
5. End with a satisfying final reveal

## Priority Series

1. Underground Secret Base
2. Abandoned Place Restoration
3. Hidden Nature House

## Avoid

Simple house construction without a secret, hidden, underground, or dramatic transformation element.
