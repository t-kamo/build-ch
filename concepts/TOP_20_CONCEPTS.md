# Top 20 AI Construction Video Concepts

## Purpose

This curated concept database selects concepts with the highest probability of generating repeatable views.

The goal is not creativity.

The goal is repeatable performance.

Each concept is selected for:

- Curiosity
- Progress
- Transformation
- Reveal
- Series expansion

## Evaluation Criteria

Each concept is scored from 1 to 5 across five factors.

| Factor | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Secret Factor | Strength of hidden, secret, underground, restricted, or impossible-location hook |
| Transformation Factor | Strength of before-to-after visual change |
| Visual Impact | Thumbnail strength and final reveal power |
| Series Potential | Ability to create many variations |
| Production Ease | Ease of producing believable image/video sequences |

Maximum total score: 25.

## Priority Categories

### Tier 1

- Hidden House
- Secret Base
- Underground Bunker

### Tier 2

- Cave House
- Tree House
- Abandoned Restoration

# First 3 Concepts To Produce

## 1. C001 — Hidden Waterfall House

### Why It Was Selected

It combines a high-curiosity natural hook with a strong fantasy reveal. The waterfall gives the concept immediate visual identity and makes the hidden entrance feel magical without becoming generic.

### Expected Strengths

- Strong first-frame hook
- High visual impact
- Clear hidden-house payoff
- Excellent series potential with cave, cliff, river, and jungle variants

### Potential Risks

- Water motion and rock consistency may be difficult across generated clips
- The entrance must remain physically believable
- The reveal needs strong lighting to avoid looking like a generic cave interior

## 2. C002 — Underground Survival Base

### Why It Was Selected

It is the cleanest repeatable construction format. The progression from empty land to hidden bunker is easy to understand and supports many future environments.

### Expected Strengths

- Very clear transformation
- High production ease
- Strong underground and secret triggers
- Easy to turn into multiple episodes

### Potential Risks

- Can feel familiar if the hook is too plain
- Needs a strong final reveal to avoid looking like a basic bunker
- Excavation stages must feel physically progressive

## 3. C003 — Cliffside Secret Base

### Why It Was Selected

It has strong visual impact and a secret-base premise that feels cinematic while still fitting the hidden construction system.

### Expected Strengths

- Powerful hook with a concealed cliff entrance
- Strong final reveal potential
- Good bridge between Hidden House and Secret Base categories
- Supports mountain, desert, ocean, and canyon variants

### Potential Risks

- Cliff geometry consistency can be challenging
- Scale may drift between exterior and interior scenes
- Needs careful image-to-image planning to keep the entrance believable
