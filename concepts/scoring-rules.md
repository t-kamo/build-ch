# Scoring Rules

Use this rubric to evaluate AI-generated construction video concepts for the Hidden Build Transformation format.

Each category is scored from 1 to 5. The maximum total score is 25.

## Evaluation Axes

| Axis | Meaning | What to look for |
|---|---|---|
| Secret | Sense of secrecy | Hidden rooms, concealed entrances, underground spaces, forbidden or private-feeling builds |
| Transformation | Amount of change | Clear before/after contrast from empty, ruined, ordinary, or impossible location to completed space |
| Visual Impact | Strength of the image | Strong reveal, unusual location, satisfying construction stages, thumbnail potential |
| Production Ease | Ease of production | Can be made with AI images/video without overly complex continuity, physics, or characters |
| Series Potential | Repeatability | Can become a repeatable episode format with variations in location, build type, and reveal |

## Score Definitions

### Secret

| Score | Rule |
|---|---|
| 1 | No secret, hidden, underground, or concealed element |
| 2 | Light privacy or secluded feeling, but not central to the concept |
| 3 | Clear hidden element, such as a concealed room or hard-to-find entrance |
| 4 | Strong secret premise that drives the whole video |
| 5 | Extremely compelling secret premise with high curiosity and click potential |

### Transformation

| Score | Rule |
|---|---|
| 1 | Minimal visual change from beginning to end |
| 2 | Some improvement, but the before/after is not dramatic |
| 3 | Clear transformation with several visible construction stages |
| 4 | Large transformation from empty, abandoned, or impossible location into a finished build |
| 5 | Extreme transformation that feels surprising, satisfying, and almost impossible |

### Visual Impact

| Score | Rule |
|---|---|
| 1 | Plain location or weak final image |
| 2 | Some visual interest, but weak thumbnail potential |
| 3 | Solid visuals with a recognizable final reveal |
| 4 | Strong cinematic setting, striking build, or highly satisfying reveal |
| 5 | Instantly clickable visual idea with a powerful first frame and final reveal |

### Production Ease

| Score | Rule |
|---|---|
| 1 | Very difficult to generate consistently; requires many characters, complex physics, or precise continuity |
| 2 | Possible, but likely to need many retries or manual fixes |
| 3 | Reasonably manageable with clear prompts and scene planning |
| 4 | Easy to generate as staged image-to-video construction shots |
| 5 | Very easy to produce repeatedly with simple locations, objects, and clear visual progression |

### Series Potential

| Score | Rule |
|---|---|
| 1 | One-off idea with limited variation |
| 2 | A few variations are possible |
| 3 | Can support a small mini-series |
| 4 | Strong repeatable structure across many locations or build types |
| 5 | Highly scalable format with many natural sequels and spin-offs |

## Recommended Interpretation

| Total Score | Priority |
|---|---|
| 22-25 | Top priority concept |
| 18-21 | Strong concept worth testing |
| 14-17 | Usable, but needs a stronger hook or reveal |
| 10-13 | Low priority |
| 5-9 | Avoid unless heavily reworked |

## Tie-breaker Rules

When two concepts have similar scores, prioritize the concept with:

1. A clearer secret or hidden element
2. A stronger first-frame hook
3. A more satisfying final reveal
4. Easier repeat production
5. Better fit with the priority series: Underground Secret Base, Abandoned Place Restoration, Hidden Nature House
