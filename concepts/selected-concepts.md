## Selection Notes

- Prioritize concepts that make the secret visible within the first 1-2 seconds.
- The first frame should show either an ordinary place, an abandoned place, or an impossible natural location.
- The middle section should show visible progress every few seconds.
- The final reveal should answer the hook with a clear hidden home, bunker, base, garage, or survival space.
- Avoid concepts where the final result is only a normal house without a hidden or transformation element.
