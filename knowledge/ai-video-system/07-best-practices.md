# Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Creating random videos.

Fix:

Build a repeatable format.

---

## Mistake 2

Skipping construction steps.

Fix:

Show logical progression.

---

## Mistake 3

Weak reveals.

Fix:

Invest more time in final scenes.

---

## Mistake 4

Changing camera angle constantly.

Fix:

Maintain consistency.

---

## Mistake 5

Generating every image from scratch.

Fix:

Use Image-to-Image.

---

## Mistake 6

Weak hooks.

Fix:

Show hidden, unusual, or unfinished states immediately.
