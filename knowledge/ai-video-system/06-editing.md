# AI Construction Video System — Editing

## Summary

Editing is responsible for:

- Maintaining attention
- Increasing retention
- Enhancing transformation
- Delivering a satisfying reveal

The purpose of editing is not cinematic storytelling.

The purpose is progression.

The viewer should constantly feel:

Something is improving.

---

# Core Editing Philosophy

Avoid:

- Fancy effects
- Heavy transitions
- Over-editing
- Excessive cinematic styling

Focus on:

- Progress
- Clarity
- Transformation
- Satisfaction

The workflow itself is the entertainment.

---

# Target Runtime

Ideal runtime:

35–45 seconds

Purpose:

Long enough to show a believable process.

Short enough to maintain retention.

---

# Winning Timeline Structure

0–3 sec

Hook

↓

3–30 sec

Build Progression

↓

30–40 sec

Transformation

↓

40–45 sec

Final Reveal

---

# Hook Optimization

Purpose:

Stop scrolling.

Requirements:

Show one of:

- Hidden entrance
- Secret location
- Underground opening
- Empty environment
- Impossible location
- Dramatic before state

The viewer should immediately ask:

"What is this?"

or

"What is being built?"

---

# Build Section

Purpose:

Create forward momentum.

Requirements:

Every clip should show visible improvement.

Avoid:

- Repetitive scenes
- Dead moments
- Waiting

The audience should constantly see progress.

---

# Speed Control

Most clips should be accelerated.

Typical scene duration:

1–3 seconds

Long scenes reduce retention.

If a clip feels slow:

Increase playback speed.

---

# Transformation Section

Purpose:

Make the audience realize the scale of change.

Requirements:

- Large visual difference
- Clear progression
- Strong contrast from earlier scenes

Examples:

Field → Bunker

Cave → Luxury Resort

Waterfall → Hidden House

---

# Final Reveal

Purpose:

Deliver satisfaction.

Requirements:

Show:

- Full space
- Lighting
- Finished environment
- Atmosphere

The reveal should feel earned.

Never rush the reveal.

---

# Scene Assembly Workflow

1. Import all generated clips

2. Arrange clips chronologically

3. Remove weak moments

4. Increase speed where necessary

5. Ensure visible progression

6. Strengthen hook

7. Strengthen reveal

8. Export

---

# Audio Strategy

Preferred:

- Natural work sounds
- ASMR
- Construction sounds

Optional:

- Light background music

Avoid:

- Loud distracting music
- Audio that hides construction sounds

---

# Platform Optimization

YouTube Shorts

- Fast hook
- Strong reveal
- High completion rate

TikTok

- Aggressive pacing
- Frequent progress

Instagram Reels

- Strong visuals
- Polished reveal

---

# Quality Checklist

Before exporting:

- Strong hook in first 3 seconds?
- Clear progression?
- No dead scenes?
- Transformation obvious?
- Reveal satisfying?
- Runtime under 45 seconds?
- Retention optimized?

---

# Common Mistakes

Avoid:

- Long intros
- Slow pacing
- Excessive transitions
- Too many effects
- Weak reveal
- Repetitive construction clips
- Scenes without visible change
