# AI Video System Knowledge Base

This directory stores reusable knowledge extracted from the AI Construction Video PDF materials.

## Files

- `01-philosophy.md`: Mindset, strategy, and operating principles for AI construction video production.
- `02-production-stack.md`: Tool stack and role definitions for ChatGPT, Nano Banana, Veo, and CapCut.
- `03-content-structure.md`: Winning content structure, hooks, transformation logic, and reveal design.
- `04-image-generation.md`: Image generation rules, start/end frame logic, and image-to-image workflow.
- `05-video-generation.md`: Video generation rules, worker consistency, timelapse, ASMR, and regeneration process.
- `06-editing.md`: Editing workflow, pacing, clip assembly, export, and publishing rules.
- `07-best-practices.md`: Common mistakes, scaling strategy, and practical production lessons.
