# Hidden Waterfall House — Image Prompts

## Episode

001 Hidden Waterfall House

## Global Environment

A massive natural waterfall located in a remote forest.

Environment must remain consistent throughout all scenes.

Maintain:

- Same waterfall
- Same cliff face
- Same terrain
- Same lighting direction
- Same scale
- Same camera position

Photorealistic style.

Ultra realistic construction detail.

## Global Camera

Maintain identical camera position, framing, perspective, lens style, and scale across the exterior waterfall sequence.

Use a cinematic static wide shot from a slightly elevated front angle, looking toward the waterfall, cliff face, misty pool, and hidden work zone behind the falling water.

For interior scenes, maintain a consistent static wide angle from inside the carved chamber, looking toward the main living area while preserving visible natural rock and waterfall context where possible.

## Global Image-to-Image Rule

Use the previous scene image as the input for the next image.

Only introduce one major construction change per scene.

Do not change weather, terrain, background, waterfall shape, cliff geometry, lighting direction, camera angle, or scale unless the scene explicitly moves from exterior to interior.
