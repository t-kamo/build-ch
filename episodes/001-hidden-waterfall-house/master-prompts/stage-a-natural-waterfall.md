# Stage A — Natural Waterfall Master Prompts

## Purpose

Generate the strongest possible base image for Episode001.

The viewer should believe this is a completely untouched natural location.

No visible clue should suggest a hidden house.

## Optimization Target

- Maximum visual beauty
- Maximum realism
- Maximum contrast with the future reveal

---

## 1. Primary Prompt

Photorealistic cinematic wide shot of a massive natural waterfall deep in a remote untouched forest wilderness. Heavy water pours down a tall dark volcanic cliff face into a misty natural pool below. The cliff is rugged, irregular, and completely natural, covered with dense green moss, wet stone texture, small plants, hanging vines, and natural mineral streaks. Thick mist rises from the impact of the waterfall near the pool. Soft daylight comes from camera left, creating realistic highlights on the falling water and subtle shadows across the volcanic rock. Static elevated front-facing camera composition, wide enough to show the full waterfall, cliff face, surrounding forest, mist, and natural pool. Ultra photorealistic, realistic scale, natural terrain, cinematic composition, high detail, grounded nature photography, no signs of human activity.

The location should feel beautiful, remote, wild, and completely untouched.

---

## 2. Alternative Prompt A

Ultra realistic nature photograph of a huge waterfall hidden deep inside a remote forest valley. A powerful curtain of water falls over a dark volcanic rock cliff into a mist-filled pool. The volcanic cliff face is raw and natural, with dense moss, wet black stone, ferns, vines, and wild vegetation growing along cracks and ledges. The scene has soft daylight from camera left, realistic water spray, humid atmosphere, and natural forest depth. Static elevated front-facing wide composition. No trail, no entrance, no cave, no tunnel, no structure, no people, no marks. The image should look like a real untouched wilderness location discovered far from civilization.

---

## 3. Alternative Prompt B

Cinematic photorealistic landscape image of a massive waterfall in an untouched remote forest. Heavy white water drops down a towering dark volcanic cliff, surrounded by dense moss, wet rock, vines, ferns, and thick wilderness vegetation. Mist floats above the natural pool at the base of the falls. Soft daylight enters from camera left, creating realistic contrast between bright water and dark volcanic stone. The camera is static, elevated, and front-facing, showing the full waterfall and cliff face with natural terrain in the foreground. The environment must feel pristine, wild, and unmodified by humans. No visible hidden opening, no cave, no tunnel, no entrance, no construction, no artificial objects.

---

## 4. Negative Prompt

No cave. No tunnel. No entrance. No doorway. No dark opening. No hidden passage. No carved rock. No rectangular shapes. No construction markings. No spray paint. No chalk marks. No flags. No stakes. No tools. No machinery. No worker. No people. No footprints. No path. No stairs. No bridge. No railing. No lights. No windows. No glass. No wood structure. No metal structure. No concrete. No building. No house. No bunker. No interior. No artificial object. No fantasy architecture. No sci-fi elements. No ruins. No temple. No resort. No visible human modification.

---

## 5. Evaluation Checklist

- Massive waterfall is the dominant visual subject
- Dark volcanic cliff is clearly visible
- Dense moss and wild vegetation feel natural
- Mist near the pool is visible and realistic
- Soft daylight comes from camera left
- Camera feels static, elevated, and front-facing
- Scene feels remote and untouched
- No cave, tunnel, entrance, dark opening, or hidden passage
- No construction markings or artificial shapes
- No human presence, tools, paths, stairs, lights, or structures
- Image is photorealistic, not fantasy or illustrated
- Composition is visually beautiful enough to serve as the opening hook
- Natural base image creates strong contrast with the future luxury reveal
