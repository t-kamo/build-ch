# Episode002 - Cliffside Workshop

## Concept

Episode002 - Cliffside Workshop

A lone craftsman discovers a narrow crack in a massive cliff and slowly builds a hidden workshop home inside it.

## Theme

"Building a home where nobody expects one."

## Production Direction

The episode should emphasize realism, manual craftsmanship, and gradual construction progress.

The build should feel achievable by one person over time.

Avoid sudden luxury jumps, oversized excavation, and impossible construction speed.
