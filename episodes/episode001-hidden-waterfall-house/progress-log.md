## Major Findings

### Finding 001

Nano Banana tends to interpret:

- entrance
- clearing
- exposed rock

as excavation activities.

Avoid wording that implies future construction intent when validating environmental consistency.

Preferred wording:

- surface cleaning only

Avoid:

- entrance preparation
- entrance area

---

### Finding 002

Nano Banana tends to over-build excavation stages.

Requested:

small human-scale excavation

Often generated:

large cave systems
pre-existing cave narratives
oversized chambers

Mitigation:

Explicitly constrain scale and describe progress as achievable by one person over time.

---

### Finding 003

Nano Banana tends to over-complete interiors when prompts contain:

- luxury
- premium furniture
- completed residence
- refined wood finishes

The model frequently jumps from:

unfinished workspace

to

fully designed lifestyle environment.

Preferred wording:

- hand-built
- practical
- self-built
- gradual progression
