# Winning Structures

## Core Structure

Before -> Build Process -> Final Reveal

## Step-by-step Formula

1. Show an empty, abandoned, or impossible location
2. Introduce a clear hidden construction goal
3. Show fast step-by-step construction
4. Increase completion level every few seconds
5. End with a satisfying final reveal

## Viral Hook Keywords

- Secret
- Hidden
- Underground
- Abandoned
- Transformation
- Impossible location

## Priority Series

1. Underground Secret Base
2. Abandoned Place Restoration
3. Hidden Nature House

## Avoid

Simple house construction without a secret, hidden, underground, or dramatic transformation element.
