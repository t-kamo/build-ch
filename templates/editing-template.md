# AI Construction Video — Editing Template

---

# Editing Objective

The audience is not watching construction.

The audience is watching transformation.

Every editing decision should increase:

- Curiosity
- Progress
- Transformation
- Satisfaction

---

Ideal Length:

35–45 seconds

- Fast pacing

# Hook Template

Purpose:

Stop scrolling immediately.

Requirements:

Show one of:

- Hidden entrance
- Mysterious location
- Empty cave
- Underground hatch
- Waterfall opening
- Abandoned structure
- Unexplained excavation

Viewer should ask:

"What is this?"

"What are they building?"

---

# Build Progression Template

Purpose:

Create momentum.

Rules:

Every scene should show visible progress.

Remove:

- Repetitive actions
- Weak clips
- Confusing sequences

Keep:

- Clear improvement
- Visible construction
- Strong progression

---

# Clip Duration Rules

Typical scene length:

1–3 seconds

Reveal scenes:

2–5 seconds

Hook scenes:

1–2 seconds

If a scene feels slow:

Increase speed.

If a scene adds no value:

Remove it.

---

# Transformation Template

Purpose:

Make the scale of change obvious.

Requirements:

Strong visual contrast between:

Start

↓

Finish

The audience should clearly see:

"Before"

and

"After"

---

# Reveal Template

Purpose:

Deliver emotional payoff.

Requirements:

Show:

- Full structure
- Finished environment
- Lighting
- Interior quality
- Atmosphere

The reveal should feel earned.

Do not rush.

---

# Audio Template

Preferred:

- Construction ASMR
- Natural sounds
- Tool sounds
- Material sounds

Optional:

- Subtle background music

Avoid:

- Overpowering music
- Distracting sound effects
